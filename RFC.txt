This is the RFC file of the Taki Bot and Front-End created by Ron, Giora, Yoni and David.
The bot:
The bot connects to the Taki server (creaed by Kfir), and when all the players are ready plays the game Taki as one of the players.
The bot assess the top pile card, the cards it has, the amount of cards its opponents have, and more, and chooses a card to play.
The bot tries to win the Taki game.
Front-End:
The Front-End shows the Taki game in a graphic layout.
It shows the cards the bot has, the amount of cards its opponents have, the top pile card, the players' moves and more.
